Level 1 SUP Instructor. Enclosed Flat Water

This foundation course is the entry level qualification for working as a stand up paddle instructor. Learn about SUP laws, instructor responsibilities, managing clients safely, creating lesson plans, instruct and assess technical skills for enclosed flat water locations. Its highly practical with time spent on the water refining skills and techniques.

BONUS: A personal SUP Paddling Video Assessment with review is included in the workshop, so you can assess your own paddling technique.

Course Information

Aim

Deliver and plan stand up paddle sessions and instruct and assess technical skills for enclosed flat water locations.

Locations

Enclosed Flat Water locations are inland waterways and bodies of water including creeks, rivers, lakes, lagoons, estuaries, canals that are no wider than 1 km, with no swell or breaking waves.

Career outcomes

Stand Up Paddle instructor in the SUP or outdoor recreation industry. Industry job roles that require SUP knowledge (e.g. marketing, sales, retail)

Topics covered

  • The role of the SUP instructor
  • Identifying laws and regulations that apply to conducting SUP lessons
  • Identifying enclosed flat water SUP lesson risks and hazards
  • Emergency processes and response
  • Logistics and how to plan enclosed flat water SUP sessions
  • Suitable boards, equipment and resources
  • Communication skills for the SUP environment
  • Instruction and coaching methods
  • Creating lesson plans
  • Technical SUP paddling skills for enclosed flat waters
  • Assessing SUP technique and providing feedback
  • Evaluating SUP sessions

Entry requirements

Before starting this course, you must:

  • Be able to understand English (equivalent to IELTS 6)
  • Have enclosed flat water paddling skills and be able to paddle safely, with confidence and correct technique. (see ASI video)
  • Enrol in, and complete the ASI SUP Wise Foundation Skills Clinic (Enclosed Flat Water)

Course requirements

At the workshop
  • Learn theory and practical SUP paddling knowledge & skills
  • Demonstrate lesson delivery on shore & water
  • Demonstrate your enclosed flat water SUP paddling skills
After the workshop
  • Complete on-line multi-choice test.
  • Complete 10 hours assisting in enclosed flat water SUP instruction at an ASI accredited or other approved SUP school.
Before or after the workshop, provide copies of
  • Personal identification (e.g drivers licence or passport)
  • Working with Children Check (as applicable to the country are working in. Information)
  • Flat Water Rescue and Senior First Aid certificate, OR Lifesaving (Combined Rescue & First Aid certificate).
Completion

You have 12 months from date of workshop to complete all course requirements.

Duration

  1. Workshop: 2 days
  2. Plus post workshop assessment & practical hours work experience

What we provide

  1. Level 1 Stand Up Paddle Instructor (Enclosed Flat Water) Training Manual
  2. SUP Instruction Logbook
  3. ASI Instructor T-shirt
  4. ASI membership valid for 12 months

Certificate issued

  1. ASI Level 1 Stand Up Paddle Instructor (Enclosed Flat Water) Certificate (valid for 12 months)

Additional certificate issued

  2. These do not expire. Available in Australia Only.
