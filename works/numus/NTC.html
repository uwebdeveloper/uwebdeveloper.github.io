Cash Payments Service from Till to Bank

An affordable, fully automated, easy to use, plug & play cash payments service from till to bank. No more counting, no more bank deposits, no more useless time spending on cash handling

The cash payment at the till is immediately booked on your account and is no longer your risk. Numus provide smart tills, smart safes, money transport services, and cloud software. Everything included for a small fee per cash transaction and no upfront investment

NTC Overview

All transactions are real time monitored and managed by NTC and can be seen immediately in the Numus Cash Portal. The cash payments are immediately booked on your account. Every moment of the day you can see your exact cash position over all your cash points and in detail per store.

Numus guarantees your cash payment as soon as it’s made and put in the till drawer. And the good thing is that you don’t have to invest in the hardware. The automatic counting drawer(s), Smart Safe, NTC, and even the cash transport, are all included in the Numus Cash Payments Service.

API’s

Numus have develop and disclosed open API’s for the manufacturers of hardware: Smart-Tills and Smart-Safe, as well as, the vendors of POS systems can easily connect their devices and software to the Numus service. Thanks to the flexibility this assortment of Api’s provide even if a retailer is already using Smart-Safes it is possible to integrate their current hardware in the Numus service.

Drivers

Numus have develop their own drivers for the proper functioning of the external hardware when connecting it with the NTC. These drivers will be keep updated as part of the Numus service. Thanks to the remote connection the process of updating can be done with no intervention of in-store employees.

Communication to the Cloud

The NTC centralized the communications between the in-store hardware and the cloud software, Numus Plaza. The operating data of the transactions, cash levels, employees, date, time, etc. Is periodically transmitted to the Numus servers where they are fully secured and can be accessed by the headquarter people of the retailer, as well as, Numus technical employees that will support the service.

Independent of the Internet

The NTC provide a new level of robustness to the Numus service. All the transactions are registered in the hard-drive of the NTC and transmitted to the Numus cloud software periodically. In case of temporary loss on Internet connection the system is able to keep running without interruption and keeping track of all activity in the hard-drive of the NTC. When the connection is reestablished all the data will be transfer to the cloud storage.

Secure

The highest standard of security has been put in the Numus service and especially in the connection with the store data and the cloud software. The NTC allow to centralize this communication and protect it with a state-of-the-art firewall. Numus Plaza also counts with the latest innovations in security, including multi-level authorization access, redundancy of data, among others.
