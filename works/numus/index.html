The Numus Cash Payments Service

Automatic Counting Till Drawer

The SMARTtill Solution is a new revolution in cash management, enabling retailers to eradicate cash losses, reduce costs associated with cash handling and increase productivity. Part of the SMARTtill Solution is an innovative new cash drawer with unique features, suitable for all retail environments anywhere in the world. It operates through integration with PoS and weighable coin and note compartments inside the drawer.

Smart Safe

The CSeXtra is a high-capacity, small footprint, smart safe designed to process cash efficiently and securely at the front line or in the back office. With each deposit, banknotes are quickly validated, and securely stored in an auto-sealing, tamper-evident, stacked bag. This system improves in-store security and builds stronger cash management procedures for the smart retailer.

Cash-in-Transit

Accredited CiT companies serve Numus clients by collecting the money in their stores and delivering change money. Armed professionals will take care of the operation following the requirements of each specific store. Security and convenience are guaranteed.

Cash Center Reconciliation

The money collected to the CiT is carried to a Cash Center. These facilities process vast amounts of money with accuracy and efficiency. This is the last physical step of the money. From here it will be repackaged or destroy, in case of unfit money

Numus Plaza (Tracking & Reporting Software)

Numus in-house develop cloud software, Numus Plaza, powers the whole service. Providing a level of visibility and optimization never seen before in cash payments. Build with the most secures security mechanism to provide access in real-time to cash-related activity to all parties involved.
