FAQ

  • What do you mean with ‘no investment’?

    We provide you the whole service, including the installation, use and maintenance of smart safes and smart tills. You don’t need to pay for those machines.

  • When can I start using it?

    After reaching out to us, we will define together what are your needs and what features are the best for you. After that we will bring you the hardware, then work on the implementation and the roll-out of the service.

  • Do I still have to manage my CIT orders?

    No. The objective of Numus is to free you up so that you only action within cash management will be skimming the cash from the smart till to the smart safe when you’re told to do so.
